# v0.4.61 - Live veto shift under ready status

## Context

The `2026-05-04` daily pipeline completed successfully and the top-level readiness artifact remained `ready`, but the live snapshot changed materially versus `2026-05-03`:

- `2026-05-03`: 19/20 live events operable, 21 total tradeable markets, no watchlist vetoes.
- `2026-05-04`: 9/20 live events operable, 6 total tradeable markets, 9 watchlist-opposed vetoes.

This is a real interpretation change even though the pipeline did not degrade operationally. The system is still healthy, but the live opportunity set is materially thinner and more copytrading-opposed than the prior day.

## Decisions

- Keep the daily pipeline interpretation at `operationally healthy`.
- Treat the live snapshot as `selective_only`, not broad-based, because veto concentration and tradable depth both deteriorated sharply day over day.
- Do not escalate to a pipeline blocker because `database_health`, audit coverage, and readiness checks all remained healthy.

## Risks and Assumptions

- The current readiness gate does not treat a sharp drop in same-day operability as a warning when database and audit coverage are still green.
- The live snapshot can therefore present a `ready` control-plane state while the execution-plane opportunity set is materially worse.
- This note assumes the day-over-day collapse from 19 operable events to 9 is large enough to matter operationally even without a hard readiness failure.
